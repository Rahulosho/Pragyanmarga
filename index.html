About Pragyanmarg

Welcome to Pragyanmarg, where inner peace meets purpose. Led by meditation expert Anand Vedant, we empower individuals through mindful practices, self-awareness, and transformational experiences. With a history of working with athletes like Olympic medalist Manu Bhakar, we bring focus, clarity, and calm to every seeker’s life journey.
