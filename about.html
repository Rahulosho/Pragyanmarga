The Story Behind
PragyanMarg

Pragyanmarg was born not from theory, but from transformation. After battling a life-threatening condition and the emotional chaos of corporate life, I, Rajat Sengupta, found healing through meditation, yoga, and spiritual exploration. What began as personal survival evolved into a calling: to share this path of mindful living, inner wisdom, and conscious manifestation.

Pragyanmarg is a home for seekers, a bridge between science and spirituality. Through it, I help others access the clarity, healing, and strength I once searched for. This isn’t just a practice, it’s a movement to awaken truth, peace, and purpose in everyday lives.
